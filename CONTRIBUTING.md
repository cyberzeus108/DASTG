## üß≠ Ground Rules

By participating in this project, you agree to:

- Follow the **[Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)**

- Prioritize **safety, hygiene, and humane treatment** over speed or aesthetics
- Avoid sharing or recommending **illegal, cruel, or high-risk practices**

If you‚Äôre unsure whether something is appropriate, open an issue or discussion first.

## üåü Ways You Can Contribute

You can contribute in many ways, including but not limited to:

### 1. Designs & Technical Drawings

- Improve existing **micro-shelter or loft designs**
- Create **new variations** for:
  - Different climates (rainy, very hot, cold)
  - Different budgets (low-cost, mid-range)
  - Different spaces (tiny corridors, rooftops, farms)
- Add or refine:
  - **Materials lists**
  - **Assembly guides**
  - **Maintenance notes**

> See: `designs/` folder structure and existing examples for reference.

---

### 2. Documentation & Guides

- Improve **clarity and readability** in Markdown files
- Add **step-by-step guides** with photos or diagrams
- Translate documentation into **local languages**
- Write **FAQ entries** based on common questions in your area

> See: `docs/`, `operations/`, `training/`, and `templates/`.

---

### 3. Operations & SOPs

- Propose better **maintenance routines**
- Share **TNR best practices** adapted to local realities
- Suggest **feeding protocols** that reduce conflict and mess
- Improve **report templates** and checklists

> See: `operations/` and `templates/`.

---

### 4. Community & Stories

- Document **local success stories** and pilots
- Add your chapter to `community/local-chapters.md`
- Write **case studies**:
  - Before/after situations
  - How you got consent
  - How conflict was handled
- Share **photos and diagrams** (respecting privacy and local laws)

> See: `community/` folder.

---

### 5. Issues, Feedback & Ideas

- Report **problems** with designs or docs
- Suggest **improvements** to workflow or structure
- Flag **safety concerns** or unclear steps
- Ask questions through GitHub Issues or Discussions

---

üìÅ File & Folder Conventions
Designs (designs/)
Keep species-specific designs in their respective folders:
designs/dogs/
designs/cats/
designs/pigeons/
Suggested file naming:
rural-micro-shelter-v1.pdf
urban-corridor-cat-shelter-v2.svg
pigeon-loft-rooftop-v1.dwg
Always include at least:
A materials list (materials-list.md or equivalent)
An assembly guide (assembly-guide.md or equivalent)
A short usage note (climate, constraints, pros/cons) if possible
Documentation & SOPs
Use Markdown (.md) for text content.
Use clear, descriptive filenames, for example:
shelter-maintenance-sop.md
tnr-guidelines.md
community-consent-process.md
Structure content with:
# for main titles
## for sections
### for subsections
Prefer simple language that non-technical people can understand.
Templates & Forms (templates/)
Keep editable templates in .md or .docx (if truly needed).
Use names like:
community-consent-form.md
monthly-report-template.md
incident-report-template.md
Prefer neutral, inclusive wording that can work in multiple regions.
üß™ Safety & Animal Welfare
If your contribution involves any advice, practice, or method that affects animals:

Ensure it is:
Humane
Non-lethal (unless under lawful veterinary guidance)
Compliant with local laws and regulations
Clearly state:
Any risks involved
Any required supervision (e.g., vet oversight)
When certain methods should NOT be used
If in doubt, open an issue or discussion first.
