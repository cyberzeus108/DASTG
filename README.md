## ğŸ¯ Why DASTG?

Street animals face:
- Conflict with humans due to unmanaged populations
- Lack of healthcare and sterilization
- Hygiene issues that affect both animals and communities
- Isolation of caregivers who feel overwhelmed

**DASTG addresses all of these** through structured, community-led, transparent systems that benefit everyone.

## ğŸ§­ Core Principles

### 1. **Iconic Micro-Shelters**
- Instagram-friendly, functional, and durable
- Designed to attract public engagement and normalize humane care
- Easy to clean and maintain

### 2. **Designated Feeding Areas**
- Clean, structured feeding zones
- Reduces conflict and hygiene issues
- Provides peace of mind for feeders and residents

### 3. **Community-Led Operations**
- Local volunteers manage shelters
- Creates micro-business opportunities
- Uses accessible tools (WhatsApp, simple forms)
- Minimal reliance on complex apps

### 4. **Humane Population Management**
- TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) for dogs and cats
- Dummy egg replacement for pigeons
- Non-lethal, ethical approaches

### 5. **Transparency & Open-Source**
- All designs and guidelines freely available
- Bank-based financial tracking
- Open reporting on progress and fund allocation
